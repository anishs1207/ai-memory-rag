Law school lecture notes

| Semester         | Core / Compulsory Subjects                                                                                                                                                    | Sample Electives / Optional Subjects                                   | Practical / Skill Components                           |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Semester I**   | - Jurisprudence / Legal Theory / Legal Method <br> - Law of Contract I <br> - Law of Torts <br> - Criminal Law I (IPC or equivalent) <br> - Family Law I                      | —                                                                      | —                                                      |
| **Semester II**  | - Evidence Law <br> - Family Law II (Personal Law) <br> - Criminal Law II <br> - Property Law <br> - Public International Law                                                 | —                                                                      | —                                                      |
| **Semester III** | - Constitutional Law I <br> - Code of Civil Procedure & Limitation <br> - Company Law <br> - Optional / Elective I <br> - Optional / Elective II                              | Media Law, Private International Law, White Collar Crimes, etc.        | —                                                      |
| **Semester IV**  | - Constitutional Law II <br> - Administrative Law <br> - Labour / Industrial Law <br> - Interpretation of Statutes & Legislative Drafting <br> - Optional / Elective III      | Gender Justice, Competition Law, Intellectual Property Rights, etc.    | —                                                      |
| **Semester V**   | - Drafting, Pleading & Conveyancing <br> - Moot Court / Internship <br> - Industrial Law (including IDRA) <br> - Principles of Taxation Law <br> - Electives                  | Information Technology Law, Criminology, International Trade Law, etc. | Moot, internships, drafting exercises                  |
| **Semester VI**  | - Advocacy, Professional Ethics & Accountancy <br> - Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) <br> - Environmental Law <br> - Jurisprudence II / Advanced Theory <br> - Electives | Insurance & Banking Law, Election Laws, Minor Acts, etc.               | Clinical legal work, arbitration simulation, legal aid |
